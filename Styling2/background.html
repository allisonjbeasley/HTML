I grew up just outside of Boulder, CO which has a strong running community. I grew up running the BolderBoulder with my dad in Elementary school and joined my first Cross Country team in middle school. I then started Track in high school in addition to Cross Country and got more competitive around this time. Now I run on Western's Cross Country and Track teams.
